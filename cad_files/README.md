These are all the CAD files I created for this project.

All screw holes are designed for M3 screws (~3mm diameter).

arm_base = desgined to be large enough to fit a Pi4B and a breadboard with extra room in all directions.

base_cover = screws on to top of arm_base

servo_wrapper = mounts 1st servo to arm_base 

second_joint_v2 = i designed this to fit on to any normal servo horn, i personally hot glued it but there are other options. 
"half moon" portion at the end is designed to distribute weight of arm assembly onto the servo_wrapper. 
large wall portion is meant to act as a spot to mount another servo_wrapper at 45 deg. i drilled holes with 9/64 drill bit because I was too lazy to cad the holes.

3rd_joint_custom_horn = this isn't a great fit, but it works. screw onto servo as you would with any other horn, but you may have to push it down some, mine was too thick.
holes at the end are for another servo_wrapper and servo.
